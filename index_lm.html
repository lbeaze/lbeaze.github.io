About

My approach to digital product design reduces costs, up-skills teams, and ensures accessible, impactful products for diverse audiences.

You need tools designed for you — ones that feel natural and intentional, moving you into confident action. I help your customers and users feel this every time they use your site, open your app, or do something in one of your physical spaces.

With 25 years of experience designing digital products for startups and Fortune 10 companies, I focus on creating scalable, intuitive solutions. My curiosity unravels complexity, delivering simple, effective designs that solve business problems and enhance user experiences.

I have taught in a Master's of Design program at the the University of Colorado, worked with international standards organizations, and led design sprints, workshops, and training.
